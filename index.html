Who

We are a few NSFS dedicated to improving the quality of life of singaporeans intending to travel overseas for any duration for work or study purposes. Our aim is to streamline the tedious administrative processes that one normally faces, through providing a common platform to proceed with such procedures. The generous help of experienced guides and various ambassadors from vital organisations will enable us to create a vibrant community wherein queries and problems can be addressed succinctly and hassle-free. We also aim to proved a platform where one can socialize with each other and continue to let the kampung spirit live in us!

What

Travelsguide is a centralised platform for your overseas needs. Autofill your required documents, contact ambassadors from key agencies like IRAS and reach out to real human mentors for advice.
